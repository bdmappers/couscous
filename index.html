How Much Cous Cous

Use this to calculate how much cous cous, oats and parmesan to pack for your camping trip. Use this for delicious food when weight and simplicity matter.

Ditch the milk powder if it's not your thing. Mix about 1/3 of your raisins into your oats, and feel free to substitute another dried fruit. Don't forget to take salt and pepper. Pro tip: pack all of these into sealable plastic bags so you're not carrying trash around with you.

*Including first and last. It is assumed that no breakfast is needed for the first day, and no dinner is needed for the last.
